title: Northwest Ridge and Lion's Way, Bugaboo Provincial Park, BC | 4th class - 600ft and 5.6 - 6 pitches

Justin has been talking up the Bugaboos for as long as I've known him. We decided it's time for me to see what it's all about! It was Justin's fifth or sixth time up to the Bugs and my first. I'm very lucky to get to follow him around.
