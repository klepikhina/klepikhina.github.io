An event packed trip to Squamish! Filled with lovely times with Fernie friends, cragging, hot-tubbing, multi-pitching, lake swimming, mountain biking with family, paddle boarding, oysters, strawberry matcha and speak-easys.

Every time we visit, I wonder when we will live here.
