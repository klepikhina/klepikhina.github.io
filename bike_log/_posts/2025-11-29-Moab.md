We camped at Utahraptor State Park with some friends that were on their own adventures. One was wrapping up a season of being a traveling bike rep; the other was off-roading Hell's Revenge.

After some coffee and breakfast burritos at Love Muffin, we rode a classic loop - up Amasa Back, towards Pothole Arch, loop Rockstacker, and down Captain Ahab.

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://klepikhina.s3.amazonaws.com/Bike/2025/November/Moab/captainahab.JPG" width="1000">&nbsp;
</p>

I don't know how I lived before my Red 40 ride snack existed.

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://klepikhina.s3.amazonaws.com/Bike/2025/November/Moab/snacks.jpg" width="500">&nbsp;
